Lifespaces For A Lifetime

“B&F Waterfront” is a gated premium villa development comprising of 7 independent river view villas each within its own compounded individual plot.

The project features aesthetic Portuguese architecture with an open to sky courtyard all built to high quality specification and superior finish. The project is located in a serene location away from the hustle and bustle of the city and yet is in close proximity to the main market area, shopping complex, restaurants and other places of interest

Amenities & Features

  • Aesthetically designed Portuguese styled Villas with center courtyard
  • Individual plots with independent villa
  • Secure gated complex
  • Complete maintenance solutions provided
  • Modular Kitchen with Hob & Chimney
  • AC fitted in all bedrooms
  • AC point provisionin the Living room & Dining along with Concealed Copper piping
  • Glass enclosure in all the bathrooms
  • Invertor provision provided
  • Hot/Cold water points in all Bathrooms and Kitchen
  • Geysers for hot water in Kitchen and all Bathrooms.
  • Sanitary ware – premium “Jaquar” fittings for the Bathrooms
  • NITCO/equivalent tiles with Black Granite Skirting
  • Teak wood main door – Frame/Shutter
  • Damp-proof treatment
  • Anti-termite treatment
